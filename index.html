Hi! I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Hebei Normal University. My research focuses on Information Retrieval, Natural Language Processing, and Large Language Models.

I am interested in building reliable and practical language-centered systems for search, understanding, and generation. I enjoy exploring research questions that connect classical retrieval techniques with modern neural and large-model methods.

I am open to academic collaboration, research discussion, and PhD related communication. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in related topics or potential collaboration opportunities.
