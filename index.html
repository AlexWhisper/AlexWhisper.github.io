About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Hebei Normal University. My work explores how retrieval and language modeling techniques can be combined to build better systems for understanding, generating, and accessing information.

I am particularly interested in information retrieval, NLP, large language models, and the evaluation of practical AI systems. I am open to academic collaboration and research communication in these areas.
