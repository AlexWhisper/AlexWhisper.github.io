About

I am a PhD student in Computer Science. My undergraduate and master's studies were completed at Hebei Normal University, and I will continue my doctoral study at Renmin University of China as a 2026 cohort PhD student.

My research interests include information retrieval, natural language processing, knowledge editing, continual learning, named entity recognition, and large language models. I am open to academic collaboration and research communication in these areas.
